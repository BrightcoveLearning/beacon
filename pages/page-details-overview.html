Viewing page details

To view the details for a page, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. Click on a page name to open the page details.

Overview

The following shows the options from the page details screen in Brightcove Beacon Studio:

Summary of options:

  • Page Status: The status of the page, either:
    • Published
    • Unpublished
    • Staged
  • Publish/Unpublish Button: Change the state of the page between published and unpublised.
  • : On click opens the page settings dialog, open to the Title & Translations section.
