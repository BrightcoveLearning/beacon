Viewing page details

To view the details for a page, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. Click on a page name to open the page details.

Overview

The following shows the options from the page details screen in Brightcove Beacon Studio:

Summary of options:

  • Page Status: The status of the page, either:
    • Published
    • Unpublished
    • Staged
  • Publish/Unpublish Button: Change the state of the page between published and unpublised.
  • : Click to open the page settings dialog, open to the Title & Translations section.
  • Current Layout: Hover over the current layout to display the Change link. Then click on Change to pick a new layout.
  • Playlist Setting Dialog: Open the playlist settings dialog by clicking the ellipsis (...).
  • Playlist Selection Panel: Click to open the playlist selection panel.
  • Availability options: Click any of the four locations to open the page settings dialog, open to the Availability section.
