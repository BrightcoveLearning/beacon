Pages Home

Pages is where you will create and manage the pages that make up your Beacon application. A list of pages that have been created will be listed in the left margin.

The first page in the list is considered the "home" page and will be displayed when the app opens. It does not have to be called "Home". Pages that are unpublished will display the unpublished icon ().

Some of the common tasks performed are:

  • Create a New Page - Click Create Page
  • Duplicate a Page - Click duplicate icon () next to page name
  • Edit a Page - Click gear icon () next to page name
  • Archive Pages - Click Archive Page
  • Change the order of pages - To change the order of pages, click on a page and drag it to a new position
  • Configure the page layout and app settings - Click gear icon () next to Pages
  • Preview the App layout - Click a device icon at the top of the page
