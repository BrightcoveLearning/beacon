Cloud Playout allows you to compile and schedule a combination of videos-on-demand and live feeds as a live linear channel.

Add new channel
  1. Click the Add Channel button.
  2. Fill in the fields and click Create Channel.
  • Details
Delete a channel
  1. Select one or more channels by clicking the check boxes.
  2. Click the button.
  3. Click in the confirmation modal to delete the channel(s) permanently.
  • Details
View/Edit Channel Details
  • Click anywhere on a channel summary to open the channel details for viewing or editing.
