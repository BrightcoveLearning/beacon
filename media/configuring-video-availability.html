Configuring Video Availability

In this topic you will learn how to configure the video availability properties for movies and episodes.

Video availability details can be used to restrict access to a videos based upon date, locations and devices. To view and edit the availability details for a video, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. Click All videos in the panel navigation.
  3. Click on a video title to open the video details page.
  4. Click the Availability tab.
Note: Availability settings are only available for videos with a Video Type of Movie or Episode.

The availability settings are controlled using rule sets. All videos must have at least one rule set. Rule sets are created to control access to a video based upon:

  • Start/end dates
  • Locations
  • Devices
For information on creating and managing rule sets, see Creating and Managing Rule Sets.
