How To: Upload Video Images

In this topic you will learn how to upload video images.

Steps

  1. Open the Media page.
  2. Click on a video title to open the video details page.
  3. Locate the IMAGES section.
  4. Hover over the poster image and click the browse icon ().
  5. Browse and select a new poster image.
  6. Hover over the thumbnail image and click the browse icon.
  7. Browse and select a new thumbnail image.
  8. For the Wide, Portrait and Ultrawide images, click the Upload new image link and browse and select an image.

Note that it may take a minute for the processing to complete and for the images to appear.
