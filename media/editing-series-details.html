Editing Series Details

In this topic you will learn how to edit series details.

To view and edit the details for a series, open the Media page and click on a series name link. The series overview page will open.

Buttons at the top of the series details page provide the ability to:

  • Publish/Unpublish the series
  • Delete the series

The series details are organized into the following tabs:

  • Overview
  • Discovery
  • Availability

Overview

The Overview tab can be used to view and edit basic series details. The following details are displayed:

  • Language View -
  • Name - No more than 255 characters.
  • Images
  • Title -
  • Synopsis -
  • Description -
  • Production Year -
  • Production Country -
  • Cast Information
    • Actors
    • Directors
    • Singers
    • Music Composers
    • Song Writers
    • Writers

Any changes made will be autosaved when you leave a field or press enter. Buttons on the right side of the page provide the ability to create a season and add a series trailer.

Discovery

The Discovery tab displays the Tags and Genres for the series.

Availability

The Availability tab can be used to restrict access to a series based upon date, locations and devices. For information, see Configuring Series Availability.
