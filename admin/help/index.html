Account information

View your Brightcove Beacon account information, including:

  • Account name
  • Account id
  • Mailing address
  • Phone number
  • Maximum number of users allowed

Edit

Edit fields associated with your account.

  1. Click Edit Account Information.
  2. Update field values.
  3. Click Save.

Notes

  • The Account ID cannot be changed.
  • Max Users is based on your account type.
