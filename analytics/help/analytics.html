Analytics

Get fast, close to real-time analytics data for your videos and players.

Performance

The performance report shows metrics, including video views, traffic sources, device types, device operating systems, and more.

  • Video Views dropdown - Select the report metrics:
    • Video Views - Number of times a video starts playback
    • Video Impressions - Number of times the video was loaded into a player and ready for interaction
    • Play Rate - Video Views divided by Video Impressions
    • Viewed Minutes - Total number of minutes the video was viewed
    • Engagement Score - Average percent watched per video view
    • Daily Unique Viewers - Average daily unique viewers for the time period selected
    • Video Bandwidth - Bandwidth for videos only; may be delayed by 3-5 days
    • Total Bandwidth - Includes bandwidth for videos, images and player loads; may be delayed by 3-5 days
  • Performance dropdown - Switch between the Performance and Engagement reports
  • Device dropdown - Select the report dimension:

    The graph shows the number of video views per day for the time period selected.
    • Video - The report body lists video name, video ID, duration and performance details for each video
    • Player - The number of times the video was loaded into a player and ready for interaction
    • Source of Traffic - Video Views divided by Video Impressions
    • Display Domain - Total number of minutes the video was viewed
    • Country - The average percent watched per video view
    • Device - The average daily unique viewers for the time period selected
    • Device Type - The bandwidth for videos only; may be delayed by 3-5 days
    • Device OS - Includes bandwidth for videos, images and player loads; may be delayed by 3-5 days
    • Device Manufacturer - The number of times a video starts playback
    • Browser Type - The number of times a video starts playback
    • Day - The number of times a video starts playback
  • Short Description - An internal description for your player
  • Player Version - Related to Update Mode. For details, see the Setting the Player Update Mode and Version document
  • Published Player Version - The current version of this player
  • Localization - Localize the player interface elements to appear in various languages
    • Default is English
    • Include the language(s) you wish to use for player localization
  • Click Save to save your changes

Engagement

The engagement report shows how users are engaging with your videos (what percentage of a video viewers are watching).

  • Playlist Type - Set how the playlist is displayed
    • Horizontal Playlist - Player will display a horizontal playlist
    • Vertical Playlist - Player will display a vertical playlist
    • Hidden Playlist - The playlist will be hidden
    Any changes made to a player may take up to 5 minutes to appear in published players.
  • Select Show title and description to display the video title and description on the player
  • Play Button Shape - Controls the shape of the play button:
    • Circle
    • Square
    • Rectangle
  • Play Button Position - Controls the location of the play button within the player:
    • Center
    • Top Left
    • Top Right
  • Color Theme - Controls the colors used in the player including the play/pause buttons, elapsed time, volume, captions, full screen and sharing icons.
  • Logo Overlay - Add an image on top of the player:
    • On - Upload an image and position it:
      • Bottom Right
      • Bottom Left
      • Top Right
    • Off - No image
  • Click Save to save your changes
